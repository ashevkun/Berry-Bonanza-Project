Berry Bonanza

Introduction

Project members : Ali Özaydin, Anastasiia Shevkun, Niklas Lifländer, Hezekiah Olarinde and Vladimir Vasilev.

Our project is about studying and analyzing data from the Natural Resources Center and other open data.

Our project aims to study this data about berries in Finland to better understand data-analytics and overall managing and working with data.

Our objectives

  • Analyze data about berries and other possible factors that can affect the berry data.
  • Create an interactive website with front-end programming which shows the data in a way that the visitor can interact with it.
  • Create a video presentation that explains how we have constructed our project in detail and what our results are.

How we complete objectives

  • We analyze the data using data analytics tools and tricks to find possible trends and patterns related to berries.
  • We carefully study materials for development of the graphical user interface of a website and implement them into our project.
  • We work on creating video presentations with voiceovers, pictures and music.

Sustainable Development Values

While working on this project, we had an intention of promoting sustainable development (SDG) values. We believe that our findings, even if only to a small extent, will help move towards more sustainable patterns of consumption and production.

The results of our analysis would help to promote positive changes in SDG 2 : Zero Hunger.By understanding data findings, it is possible to outline factors that have been affecting berry harvest in different parts of Finland for the past few years. It resonates with UN's goal to help maintain ecosystems, as well as strengthen capacity for adaptation to climate change.

By affecting that area, we are also contributing to SDG 12 : Responsible Consumption and Production. It is essential to manage our natural resources efficiently, and our project team believes the results of the conducted analysis would contribute to changing the way we produce and consume resources.
